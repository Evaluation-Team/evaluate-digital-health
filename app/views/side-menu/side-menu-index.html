Evaluating Digital Health Products

Evaluation is the process of collecting information about a product or service and using this to judge its effectiveness.

We've created these resources to help you carry out evaluations, either in your team or with external evaluators. They are intended for anyone developing or running a digital health product who wants to know about evaluation. Digital health products can take different forms – for example, a service, campaign or other intervention. They might be provided through an app, a website, or a combination of services provided through technology and in person.

It’s important to conduct an evaluation for all digital health products. Evaluation will help you demonstrate what impact your product has on health outcomes and whether it is cost-effective. It can provide you with useful information about how to make your service better, including what works and what doesn’t.
