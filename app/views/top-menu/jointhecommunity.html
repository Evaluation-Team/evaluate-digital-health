Join the evaluation community

The evaluation community is a place where you can:

  • ask questions about evaluation and develop your knowledge
  • share good practice and advice
  • stay updated on the evaluation work that different departments are involved in
  • meet people across government with an interest in evaluation

Community channels

  • KHub, for discussions with colleagues who work in central or local government
  • Slack, for discussions with colleagues across government, as well as those who are working in the private and third sectors

Events

We held our first evaluation meetup in February 2019. We’re looking to hold more meetups to help grow an evaluation culture.
